    Research & training data

    This app combines literature-derived context prototypes (Molnár et al. 2008; Yin & McCowan 2004; Pongrácz et al. 2005) with a logistic-regression emotion model trained on EmotionalCanines. Train or refresh the model with python ml/train_emotion_classifier.py.

    • Yin, S., & McCowan, B. (2004). Barking in domestic dogs: context specificity and individual identification. Animal Behaviour.
    • Molnár, C., et al. (2008). Classification of dog barks: a machine learning approach. Applied Animal Behaviour Science.
    • Pongrácz, P., et al. (2005). Acoustic parameters of dog barks carry emotional information for humans. Applied Animal Behaviour Science.
    • Dang, T. M., et al. (2025). EmotionalCanines: A Dataset for Analysis of Arousal and Valence in Dog Vocalization. ACM MM '25.
    • Lekhak, H., et al. (2025). DogSpeak: A Canine Vocalization Classification Dataset. ACM MM '25.
